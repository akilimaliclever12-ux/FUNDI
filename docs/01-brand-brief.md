# Brand Brief — Fundi Bukavu (working name)

> Foundation document · Market: Bukavu, DRC · Audience: mobile-first, WhatsApp-heavy, low-bandwidth

---

## 1. Brand Name Suggestions

"Fundi" means *skilled craftsman / expert* in Swahili — instantly understood across the eastern DRC and Great Lakes region. Strong candidates:

| Name | Rationale | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| **Fundi** | Short, local, memorable. One word = strong brand. | Check trademark/domain. Possibly too generic alone. |
| **FundiLink** | "Fundi" + connection. Communicates the matchmaking role. | Clear, scalable beyond Bukavu. |
| **Fundi Bukavu** | Geographic trust anchor for launch city. | Great for launch, limits national expansion later. |
| **MaFundi** | "My craftsmen" — ownership and warmth. | Friendly, plural implies choice. |
| **Fundi Yangu** | Swahili for "my craftsman." | Personal, local feel. |
| **Kazi Bora** | Swahili for "good work / quality work." | Speaks to outcome, not just people. |
| **Stadi** | From *ustadi* (skill/mastery). | Short, modern, app-like. |

**Recommendation:** Launch as **Fundi** (with "Bukavu" as a launch-market descriptor — *Fundi · Bukavu*). It is local, trusted, one syllable-cluster, easy to say on a phone call, and expandable to other cities later (*Fundi Goma*, *Fundi Kinshasa*).

## 2. Mission

> **Make it fast and safe for anyone in Bukavu to find a trusted skilled worker — and help skilled workers earn more by reaching more clients.**

---

## 3. Vision

> To become the trusted home of skilled labor across the DRC and the Great Lakes region — where every reliable electrician, plumber, mason, or welder can build a reputation that travels, and every household or business can hire with confidence.

---

## 4. Core Values

1. **Trust first** — Verified workers, real reviews, no fake profiles.
2. **Dignity of work** — We elevate the trades; a fundi is a professional, not a last resort.
3. **Simplicity** — If a feature doesn't help someone hire or get hired, it doesn't ship.
4. **Accessibility** — Works on cheap phones, weak networks, and through WhatsApp.
5. **Local roots** — Built for Bukavu's neighborhoods, languages, and realities.
6. **Fairness** — Transparent pricing, no exploitation of workers or customers.

---

## 5. Target Audience

**Primary — Customers (demand side)**
- Homeowners and renters needing repairs (electrical, plumbing, leaks, broken fixtures).
- Small business owners (shops, restaurants, salons) needing fit-outs and maintenance.
- People building or renovating homes who need masons, welders, carpenters, painters.
- Demographic: 25–55, urban Bukavu, smartphone owner, WhatsApp-native, French/Swahili speaking, price-sensitive, time-poor.

**Primary — Workers (supply side)**
- Skilled tradespeople: electricians, plumbers, carpenters, masons, welders, painters, general construction.
- Often find work today only through word-of-mouth and physical presence at hardware markets.
- Smartphone owners but data-conscious; comfortable on WhatsApp, less so on formal apps.
- Want: more steady clients, fewer idle days, a credible reputation.

**Secondary**
- NGOs, contractors, and property managers sourcing reliable labor at scale.
- Diaspora paying for work on family property back home.

> For **households and businesses in Bukavu** who need **reliable skilled workers but struggle to find trusted ones**, **Fundi** is a **mobile-first directory and connection platform** that **lets you find, vet, and contact verified tradespeople in minutes — directly over WhatsApp**. Unlike **word-of-mouth or random Facebook posts**, Fundi gives you **verified profiles, real customer reviews, and a single trusted place to hire**.

If Fundi were a person: a **respected local master craftsman** — experienced, honest, calm, and approachable. Knows everyone, vouches only for good work, speaks plainly, never overpromises.

Archetype: **The Everyman + The Sage** — trustworthy, grounded, knowledgeable, unpretentious.

## 12. Color Palette Recommendations

**Brand direction: a black → blue gradient.** Modern, premium, high-tech and trustworthy — reads beautifully on phone screens and stands out against the bright, dusty backdrops common in Bukavu. The gradient is the signature; supporting neutrals keep content legible and bandwidth low.

### Signature gradient
| Role | Definition | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| **Brand gradient** | `linear-gradient(135deg, #0A0F1F → #0B5FFF)` | Hero sections, primary buttons, headers, splash. The brand's core identity. |
| **Gradient (deep)** | `linear-gradient(135deg, #000000 → #0A2540)` | Darker, subtle variant for large backgrounds/footers. |
| **Gradient (vivid)** | `linear-gradient(135deg, #0B5FFF → #3B82F6)` | Accent highlights, active states, progress. |

### Solid tokens (for text, borders, fills)
| Role | Color | Hex | Why |
|------|-------|-----|-----|
| **Brand black** | Near-black Ink | `#0A0F1F` | Gradient start, headers, primary text on light. |
| **True black** | Black | `#000000` | Deep backgrounds, max contrast. |
| **Primary blue** | Electric Blue | `#0B5FFF` | Gradient end, links, primary accents. |
| **Blue bright** | Sky Blue | `#3B82F6` | Hover/active, secondary accents. |
| **Blue glow** | Soft Blue | `#60A5FA` | Subtle highlights, icons on dark. |
| **Neutral light** | Off-white | `#F7F8FA` | Light backgrounds, low eye strain. |
| **Neutral mid** | Slate Gray | `#6B7280` | Secondary text. |
| **Neutral dark** | Charcoal | `#1F2937` | Body text on light. |
| **Success / WhatsApp** | WhatsApp Green | `#25D366` | Keep distinct for "Contact on WhatsApp" buttons. |
| **Warning** | Amber | `#F59E0B` | Pending verification, alerts. |
| **Error** | Red | `#DC2626` | Errors, rejections. |

### Usage rules
- **Light theme by default** (better readability on slow/bright outdoor screens): white/off-white surfaces, dark ink text, gradient reserved for the hero, primary CTAs, and key headers.
- **Gradient sparingly** — it's the signature, not the wallpaper. Use flat `#0B5FFF` for most interactive elements to keep things light and fast.
- **WhatsApp green stays green.** Don't fold the primary conversion button into the gradient — keep it instantly recognizable.
- **Text on gradient** is always white/off-white; verify ≥4.5:1 contrast at the lighter (`#0B5FFF`) end.
- Implement as Tailwind tokens, e.g. `bg-gradient-to-br from-[#0A0F1F] to-[#0B5FFF]`.

**Accessibility:** maintain WCAG AA contrast (≥4.5:1) for text. The black→blue range is colorblind-safe; always test white text against the *lightest* part of any gradient, not the darkest.

## 13. Typography Recommendations

Optimize for **fast load** (system/variable fonts), legibility on small screens, and good support for French accents.

| Use | Font | Fallback | Notes |
|-----|------|----------|-------|
| **Headings** | **Inter** (or Poppins for warmth) | system-ui, sans-serif | Geometric, modern, free, excellent accent support. |
| **Body** | **Inter** | -apple-system, Segoe UI, Roboto, sans-serif | Highly legible at small sizes, variable font = fewer bytes. |
| **Numbers/UI** | Inter (tabular figures) | — | Phone numbers, ratings. |

**Rules**
- Prefer **one font family** (Inter) with weights 400/500/600/700 to minimize download size.
- Use `font-display: swap` and self-host or use `next/font` to avoid layout shift and external blocking.
- Base size **16px**; never below 14px for body. Generous line-height (1.5) for readability.
- Avoid loading multiple weights you don't use — every KB matters on 3G.
